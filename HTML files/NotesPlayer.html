Play with Notes

Note
Use these notations

S, R, G, m, P, D, N for pure notes (shudhh swar)

r, g, d, n for soft or flat notes (komal swar)

M for sharp Ma (teevra swar)

Use '+' sign for Higher octave (Taar saptak). E.g.: S+

Use '-' sign for Lower octave (Mandra saptak). E.g.: S-

Do not use any sign for Medium octave (Madhya saptak). E.g.: S

Use any special character to give a pause of 0.5 seconds(one special character will create 0.5 seconds delay)

Provide a space after every note otherwise it will be ignored.
